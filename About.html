About our Professional Cleaners

Kelley's Homecare was founded right in the heart of Auburn in January of 2019. Mrs Kelley started cleaning from home to home with only herself to hire. Kelley's Homecare is built on pure passion and desire to help others live a worry free life by taking care of their home for them. We strike for the upmost satisfaction, no matter what the service is. You can expect the same friendly face for nearly every oppointment, which ensures trust and personal cleaning. We prioritize based on individual desires with every client. We use all our own safe-non harsh products to maintain all your surfaces and appliances.
